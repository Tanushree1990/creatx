Sustainable Transportation




Develop and promote sustainable transportation systems, such as improved public transit networks, cycling infrastructure, and pedestrian friendly streets, to reduce traffic congestion, air pollution, and greenhouse gas emissions.

Affordable Housing


Increase the availability of affordable housing options for low-income populations by implementing inclusive housing policies, supporting community land trusts, and promoting innovative financing mechanisms.

Green Building and Energy Efficiency

Encourage the construction of green buildings that are energy-efficient, use sustainable materials, and incorporate renewable energy technologies to reduce carbon emissions and minimise the environmental impact of urban development.
